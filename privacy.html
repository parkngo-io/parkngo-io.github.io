Website Visitors

Like most website operators, ParknGo collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. ParknGo’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how ParknGo’s visitors use its website. From time to time, ParknGo may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

ParknGo also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http://www.parkngo.io blog posts. ParknGo only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, ParknGo uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. ParknGo uses cookies to help ParknGo identify and track visitors, their usage of http://www.parkngo.io, and their website access preferences. ParknGo visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using ParknGo’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of ParknGo’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to ParknGo's use of cookies.
